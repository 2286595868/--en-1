Shenzhen E-Lannet Technology Co., Ltd

Shenzhen E-Lannet Technology Co., Ltd, which is formerly the Shenzhen Yidelai Electronic Co., Ltd established in 2001, was established in March, 2013 as a professional company specializing in EMS flexible manufacturing services. We can provide EMS one-stop manufacturing services, including PCB design, material procurement, SMT / PCBA, assembly and testing. We independently developed the first industry-leading detection system which could provide the accurate verdicts of the design scheme and that could provide complete and accurate First Article Inspection (FAI) reports in which contents include the measurement value of each component in each position and the abnormal material report. Obtained ISO9001, ISO14001, ISO13485 (for medical equipment), TS16949 (automotive electronics) certifications, etc.
